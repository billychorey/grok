GROK is an event unlike any other.

Don't be a stranger in a strange land. Instead of listening to speakers, GROK is a gathering of creative people who want to connect with their peers and discuss the most pressing issues of our day in group discussions called 10/20s.

REGISTER

What are 10/20s?

10/20s are mediated discussions between about a dozen participants. Any participant in a 10/20 can propose a topic for discussion. You can talk about software, your work environment, your personal challenges–anything that’s on your mind.

Session Leaders

At GROK, everyone is welcome, and we invite some of the best designers and thinkers in the industry to mediate the 10/20s and bring their experience and insight to every conversation.

In the past, we've enlisted mediators like Joshua Blankenship, Matt Donovan, Paul Armstrong, Marco Suarez, Jackson Alexander, Ryan McCrary, James White, Johnny LeHane, Pamela Pavliscak, Richard Banfield, & Carl Smith to bring their experience and insight to every conversation.

Moldover

(musician)

If technology and music are your life, brace yourself- MOLDOVER is about to reformat your soul. Hailed by over 1,000,000 YouTube viewers as "The Godfather of Controllerism", Moldover is a new breed of music icon. Combining the charisma of a rock star, the mad genius of a basement inventor, and the radical inclusiveness of the DIY internet generation, Moldover is "literally throwing away the rule book and reinventing the wheel" (Remix Magazine). Experience one of his JAMBOX multiplayer instruments at a festival, or pick up and play the "LIGHT THEREMIN" packaging of his debut album and understand why the next paradigm for music is a one-man-brand called Moldover.
